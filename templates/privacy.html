Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, post a job, or apply for a position. This includes your name, email address, password hash, and any profile or job-related data you submit.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to operate and maintain JobPortal, including:

  • Facilitating the job search and application process.
  • Allowing employers to manage listings and applicants.
  • Communicating with you about your account and platform updates.
  • Improving our services and user experience.

3. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access. This includes using hashed passwords and secure database configurations.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only as necessary to facilitate the platform's core functions (e.g., sharing a seeker's application data with the respective employer).

Questions?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@jobportal.com.
